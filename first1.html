VR GAMING

VR gaming is the term used to describe a new generation of computer games with virtual reality technology that gives players a truly immersive, first-person perspective of game action (VR) Participants both experience and influence the game environment through a variety of VR gaming devices and accessories, including VR headsets, sensor-equipped gloves, hand controllers, and more.



Hologram Video Games

We use the word 'holographic' in the title as that's what people think of when they see it. It's really a 3D volumetric display that allows you to view the game from all angles. It's almost impossible to capture how good it looks on video when going from 3D back to a 2D screen. You just have to see it in person!
